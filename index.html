About Nggc

Nggc is a welcoming community dedicated to sharing the love of Christ and making a positive impact in our city. Our mission is to create a space where people can experience God, grow in their faith, and serve others.

Our Pastors

Meet our dedicated pastoral team, committed to guiding our congregation with wisdom, compassion, and a deep love for God's word. They are here to support you in your spiritual journey and help you connect with our church family.

We believe in creating a church that feels like home, where everyone is valued and encouraged to grow in their relationship with Jesus Christ.

Meet Our Pastors

Our pastors are dedicated to shepherding our flock with love and biblical truth.
